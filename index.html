Hi, fellow human! I am Ishita, the mind behind Vivid and Grey. I am an artist and a visual designer currently living a freelancer's life in coastal Goa, India.

My design work over the years has been multifaceted, ranging from contract illustration work to full time graphic design. I've worked with clients from different industries and parts of the world. This diversity is a reflection of my pursuit of the new, seeing each design as a voyage and a chance to give my best effort. If my work looks like a good fit for your project (or if you wish to say hi!), please email at ishita.nager@outlook.com

Other than professional design services, my interests lie in life, the universe, and everything. I spend my time creating art that breathes both the 'vivids' and the 'greys' of the human experience and that of nature. Through these themes of duality and depth, I craft experiences that resonate with the soul and kindle a shared sense of wonder, acceptance, and appreciation. You can choose to take home some of my artwork from the original art section or ping me for a custom order.

As a hobby, I create atmospheric AI art and build imaginative short stories around them for my tiny youtube channel. Else, I can be found embracing a challenge (like developing this website from scratch!), or, lost in either the sunset's hue or Claire de Lune.
